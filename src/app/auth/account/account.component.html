Account Settings Basic Account Information Opens Keycloak account management. You can change your password, login provider and 2FA over there. Furthermore, you can delete your account. This will immediately delete any content you have created. If you subscribed, this will also terminate your subscription so that no further payments will occur. Manage Account Subscription Opens external payment provider website. You can subscribe, change your payment method or cancel the subscription over there. Manage Subscription Account Data Will delete all your created content. That means timetables, formations, routes and everything linked to it. This is irrevocable. Delete all user-generated content
